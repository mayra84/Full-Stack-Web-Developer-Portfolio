Pawsibilities

For this project, I was a member of a 2-person team where we built an app to help pet owners track their dog's health daily. We wanted to give pet owners' the opportunity to provide the best possible health care for their furry loved one.
My responsibilities included designing a cohesive layout, creating the backend to store user and dog information, and incorporating AWS S3 for image storage.
This app was built with React, Redux, JavaScript, Express, Sequelize, PostgreSQL, Beekeeper, Postman, AWS, Google Maps API, Weather API, and Chakra.

Body in Mind

Solo project where I built an app centered around mental health.
The purpose of this app was to encourage people struggling with their mental health to schedule physical activities for themselves in advance. By scheduling some small activities, they would have time to mentally prepare and maybe even look forward to them, all while keeping their Body in Mind.
The objective of this project was to create a project using React and Redux. Built with JavaScript, React, Redux, and Chakra.

MnZ Movie Travel

Member of a 3-person development team building a website where users explore movie and tv show filming locations and can access their real-time webcam feed.
Because travel may not be accessible to all, MnZ Travel provides everyone, regardless of circumstance, to visit places all over the world with the click of a button.
Responsibilities included accessing several APIs' to render information, and designing and assembling a fully mobile-responsive layout.
This app was built with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript
